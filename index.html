Southern Mac & Cheese

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Sharp Cheedar Cheese 1/2 cup
Colby or Montery Jack 1/2 cup
Mozzarela 1/2 cup
American Cheese 1/2 cup
Cream Cheese 1/2 cup
Garlic Powder 1/4 teaspoon
Mustard Powder 1/4 teaspoon
Smoked Paprika 1/4 teaspoon
Macaroni/Elbow Pasta 8 oz
Butter 7 tablespoons
Breadcrumbs 2/3 cup
Milk 3 cups
Flour 1/3 cup

Step 1:Preheat oven to 180°C/350°F (all oven types).

Step 2:Make roux: In a large saucepan or in an ovenproof skillet (I use my 26cm/9" Lodge cast iron skillet), melt butter over medium heat. Add flour and cook, stirring constantly, for 1 minute.

Step 3:Add milk: Add about 1 cup of the milk and mix to dissolve the paste into the milk. Then add remaining milk and mix until lump free (use whisk if required).

Step 4:Add Seasonings: Mix in salt and Seasonings if using.

Step 5:Thicken sauce: Cook, stirring/whisking regularly, for 5 through 8 minutes until thickened to a cream consistency. When the Sauce coats the back of a wooden spoon, you should be able to draw a path with your finger.

Step 6:Add cheese: Remove from stove, add cheese and stir cheese doesn't need to melt.

Step 7:Check salt: Adjust salt to taste (if you use recommended cheeses, you won't need more)

Step 8:Assemble: Pour Sauce into pot with Macaroni. Stir quickly, then pour back into the skillet or a baking dish. Sprinkle with breadcrumb topping.

Step 9:Bake for 25 minutes or until top is light golden. Don't bake too long otherwise you'll bake away the Sauce.

Step 10:Serve: Serve immediately! I sprinkled mine with a bit of fresh parsley. See notes for best make-ahead method.
